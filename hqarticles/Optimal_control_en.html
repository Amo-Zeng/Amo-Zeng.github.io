Introduction

Optimal control theory is undergoing a profound paradigm shift. Traditional methods relying on linear assumptions, deterministic models, and stationary noise struggles to address complex system challenges like multiscale dynamics, non-stationary uncertainties, and heterogeneous hierarchical coupling. This paper establishes a unified theoretical framework integrating biological regulation, quantum-classical hybrid control, and neural decoding.
