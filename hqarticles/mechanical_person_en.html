Global Regulatory Policies

  • EU GDPR (2018): Encompasses 51 core articles, granting users the "right to be forgotten".
  • US CCPA (2020): Requires businesses to disclose data usage and allows users to opt-out of data sales.
  • China PIPL (2021): Strictly regulates cross-border data transfers, similar to GDPR but operates independently.
#### Terms Explanation

Federated Learning is a distributed machine learning approach where models are trained on local devices (e.g., mobile phones) without uploading data, thereby enhancing privacy protection.
