隐私保护新技术

差分隐私

通过向数据集添加随机噪声，差分隐私确保单个数据无法被逆向推导，同时保留全局统计价值。

联邦学习

模型在本地设备上训练，而非集中传输数据，显著降低隐私泄露风险。

Differential Privacy

Adding random noise to datasets ensures individual data cannot be re-identified while preserving global statistical value.

Federated Learning

Models train locally on devices without centralized data transmission, drastically reducing privacy risks.

  • Decentralization: Reduces single data storage points, avoiding attack targets.
  • Real-time Response: Edge devices process data locally (e.g., autonomous vehicles), reducing latency.
  • Data Localization: Sensitive info stays local, complying with GDPR/CCPA.
