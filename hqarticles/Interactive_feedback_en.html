Interactive Feedback: The Emotional Bond and Behavioral Guidance Between Humans and Technology

In the digital age, the way humans interact with technology is undergoing profound transformation. From smartphones to smart homes, from virtual reality to AI assistants, every click, swipe, and voice command constitutes a complex network of human-computer interactions. In this process, interactive feedback serves as a crucial bridge connecting users with systems, not only determining the smoothness of the user experience but also subtly shaping users' behavioral patterns.
