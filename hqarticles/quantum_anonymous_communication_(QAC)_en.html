Quantum Anonymous Communication: Future of Privacy Protection and Security Challenges

With the rapid development of information technology, privacy protection and communication security have become global focal points. Traditional anonymous communication methods, such as the Onion Router (Tor), while providing a certain level of privacy protection, still face risks of being tracked and cracked by attackers. The development of quantum mechanics brings new possibilities for anonymous communication, but also introduces new challenges. This article will explore the future of quantum anonymous communication from three perspectives: achieving true anonymity through quantum entanglement, the threat of quantum computing to traditional anonymous communication, and the construction of decentralized quantum anonymous communication networks.
