Social Change in Saudi Arabia: From Closed to Open

Saudi Arabia, a land rich in oil resources and deep cultural heritage, has undergone profound social changes in recent years. Transitioning from a traditional "closed society" to an "open society," Saudi Arabia has demonstrated unique wisdom and strategies in balancing traditional values with modernization. This article will explore how Saudi Arabia is building a more inclusive and diverse society by enhancing women's status, promoting education reform and international exchange, and integrating traditional culture with modern elements.
