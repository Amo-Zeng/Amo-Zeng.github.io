Breakthrough Rebirth: The Phoenix of Performing Arts and the Return to Essence

Boundary-breaking performance is not merely an innovation in technique but a re-recognition of the essence of performance. The boundaries between performance and life are dissolving. This dissolution is not merely the merging of art into life or vice versa but a profound understanding of the essence of performance.
