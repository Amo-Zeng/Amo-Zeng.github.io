The Innovative Path of Environmental Education: The Integration of Gamification, Storytelling, and Community Engagement

In today's rapidly advancing digital age, traditional methods of environmental education seem somewhat dull and uninspiring. Young people are more inclined to acquire knowledge and skills through interactive and engaging means. Therefore, integrating environmental education with gamification, storytelling, and community engagement not only enhances the fun of learning but also sparks their interest and passion for environmental protection.
