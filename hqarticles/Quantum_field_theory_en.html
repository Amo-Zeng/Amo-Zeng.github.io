Exploring the Unified Paradigm of Quantum Field Theory

Abstract

Quantum Field Theory (QFT) is transcending its traditional boundaries in particle physics to develop a unified interpretive paradigm across scales, dimensions, and disciplines. This paper systematically demonstrates three breakthrough applications of QFT: (1) Revealing vacuum as a non-local information encoding medium through AdS/CFT duality and holographic principles; (2) Constructing low-dimensional field theories as "inverse probes" for high-dimensional physics based on "dimensional inversion" phenomena between low-dimensional exact solutions and high-dimensional strong coupling problems; (3) Introducing scalar field symmetry breaking, gauge field propagation, renormalization groups, and Feynman diagram techniques to living system modeling...
