Data Privacy Protection Strategies

  1. Local Data Processing

    Deploy a federated learning framework where data is used for model training at the edge devices, without leaving the local servers.

  2. Differential Privacy Protection

    Inject noise into data analysis to ensure individual data characteristics cannot be tracked; widely applied in public safety surveillance systems.

  3. Blockchain Logging

    Use distributed ledger technology to record data access logs, ensuring the traceability and immutability of medical data retrieval.
