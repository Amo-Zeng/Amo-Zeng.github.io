Artificial Intelligence and Data Privacy: Balancing Innovation with Protection

The Impact of Artificial Intelligence on Data Privacy

  • Data Collection: AI applications may collect data without explicitly informing users.
  • Data Misuse: Collected data may be used beyond the original purpose, or even misused by third parties.
  • Risk of Re-identification: Even anonymized data sets can be re-identified when combined with other data sources.

Strategies for Protecting Data Privacy

Differential Privacy

Protects individual data by adding mathematical noise to it, making it untraceable.

Federated Learning

Trains models at the data source rather than centralizing the data itself.
