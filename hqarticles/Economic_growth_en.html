Artificial Intelligence and Data Privacy

Balancing Technological Innovation and Privacy Protection

As machine learning models require massive amounts of training data, the risk of user privacy breaches has grown exponentially. This article deeply analyzes the five key security threats and the construction of a three-tiered defense system across the data lifecycle.

Three Major Challenges

Data Collection Transparency

68% of global companies fail to clearly state the scope of data use, triggering GDPR compliance risks

Feature Reconstruction Attacks

Attackers can reverse-engineer private user features through model outputs, with MIT case studies showing a 78% success rate

Cross-Model Correlation Leaks

Implicit data association risks when using multiple models together, commonly seen in healthcare and finance
