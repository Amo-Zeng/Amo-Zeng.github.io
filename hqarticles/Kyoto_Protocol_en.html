Three Key Areas of Data Privacy Protection

  • Technical Safeguards: Differential Privacy, Federated Learning, Homomorphic Encryption.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Standards like GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and China's PIPL (China Personal Information Protection Law).
  • Ethical Governance: Ethical principles such as those from MIT – focusing on transparency, accountability, and fairness.
