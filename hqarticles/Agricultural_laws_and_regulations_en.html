Problem Identification: Paradigm Challenges and Transition Opportunities in Agricultural Governance

Global agricultural governance faces a paradox between digital revolution and ecological crisis. FAO data (2023) shows that while agricultural algorithm decision systems cover 37% of global arable land in 2023, related legal disputes have increased by 112% annually, revealing three structural contradictions in traditional agricultural legal systems:

  • Technological Regulatory Lag: "Algorithmic regulation gaps" in cases like intelligent agricultural machinery infringement
  • Fragmentation of Ecological Values: Obstacles in ecological value conversion due to missing carbon sink pledge mechanisms
  • Social Contract Fragmentation: Systematic exclusion of smallholders by standardized contracts
