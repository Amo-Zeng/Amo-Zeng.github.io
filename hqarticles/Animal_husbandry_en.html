Ⅰ. Systemic Domination: When Animal Husbandry Meets Digital Capitalism

In the triple helix of data, capital, and life, modern animal husbandry witnesses the explosive manifestation of Foucault's "biopower" theory. FAO 2023 data reveals 76% of global livestock production is now integrated into electronic monitoring networks, with algorithms converting life into computable "biological components" by collecting 14MB/day/cow data (DairyTech 2024).

‖ Theoretical Nexus ‖

Bourdieu's "symbolic violence" explains: When sensor-equipped cows become data sources, technological permeation obscures capital's asymmetrical appropriation of life-information.
