O2O Integration: New Models, New Values, New Future

In the digital era, O2O (Online-to-Offline) integration is not just a business model but also a social development trend. By promoting resource sharing and information transparency, O2O integration can drive social equity and sustainable development. This article will delve into how O2O integration brings positive changes to society from the perspectives of experience continuity, data-driven approaches, and social value.
