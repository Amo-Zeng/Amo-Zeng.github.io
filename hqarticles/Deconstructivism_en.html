Introduction: Technological Embodiment of Deconstructionism - The Cognitive Revolution in the Posthuman Era

At the intersection of post-phenomenology and the material turn, deconstructionism is undergoing a profound techno-ontological shift. Unlike the 20th-century subversion of textual structures, contemporary deconstructionism redefines technology's role in cognitive construction through concrete practices like algorithmic decision-making, digital embodiment, and quantum measurement.
