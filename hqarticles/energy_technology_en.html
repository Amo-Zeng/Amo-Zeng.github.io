From "Energy Technology" to "Energy Future"

Introduction

The development of energy technology has always been a crucial driving force for the progress of human society. From fossil fuels to renewable energy, each innovation in energy technology has profoundly changed the way we live and the structure of our economy. However, in the face of increasingly severe climate change and resource depletion, existing energy technologies are no longer sufficient to meet the demands of sustainable development. This article will explore the direction of future energy technology development, analyze the possible forms of the future energy system, and contemplate the profound impact of these technological advancements on human society.
