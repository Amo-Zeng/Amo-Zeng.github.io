The "Democratization" of Data Warehouses

The "Democratization" of Data Warehouses: From "Tools for the Few" to "Data Assets for All"

Data is hailed as the "oil" of the new era, and its value is self-evident. However, for a long time, the data warehouse—which carries the heavy responsibility of data storage, processing, and analysis—has been like a "temple" towering above, accessible only to a select few professionals such as data scientists and analysts who can mine its value. This "tool for the few" model not only limits the maximization of data value but also hinders the digital transformation process of enterprises.
