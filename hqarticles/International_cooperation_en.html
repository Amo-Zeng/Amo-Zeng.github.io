A New Paradigm for International Cooperation: Symbiosis, Technology Empowerment, and Cultural Integration

Driven by the wave of globalization, international cooperation is at an unprecedented historical juncture. Traditional thinking in international relations is no longer capable of addressing the complex and ever-changing global challenges of today. The construction of a new cooperation model has become an inevitable choice of the times. This new model not only emphasizes the concept of symbiosis and mutual benefit but also relies on the power of technology to empower cooperation. Moreover, it requires deep cultural integration to jointly promote international cooperation towards a more closely connected, sustainable, and inclusive future.
