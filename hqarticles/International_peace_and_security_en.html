Order vs. Chaos: The Triple Dialectics in the Era of Technological Governance

Introduction: The Meta-Contradiction of Technological Governance - The Symbiosis of Order Construction and Innovation Suppression

Technology, as the "double helix" of human civilization, inherently combines order construction with innovation suppression. This paper analyzes three representative domains:

  • Micro-scale: Standardized control of neuronal populations through Batch Normalization (BN)
  • Meso-scale: Geopolitical games triggered by climate change ("securitization of climate")
  • Macro-scale: Biosecurity governance dilemmas caused by gene editing technologies

Through a three-dimensional analytical framework of "control-game-ethics," we reveal the universal dialectic in technological governance: all order construction inevitably accompanies certain degrees of freedom suppression, while absolute freedom leads to systemic collapse.
