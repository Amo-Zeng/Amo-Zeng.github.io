Introduction: From "Inflammation-centric Theory" to Dynamic Systems Paradigm

Neuroimmunology is undergoing a cognitive revolution from mechanical reductionism to complex systems research. Traditional cytokine-centric inflammation models are gradually being superseded by the "Metabolism-Immune-Sensing" (MIS) triaxial integration framework. This paradigm establishes a unified theoretical system for neurodegenerative diseases, neuroimmune disorders, and central nervous system tumor immunotherapy through energy flux reprogramming, immune dynamics phase transitions, and cross-scale multi-omics sensing.
