Introduction: The Paradigm Shift in Web Programming

With the explosive development of 5G/6G, metaverse, and affective computing technologies, network systems are undergoing a paradigm upgrade from "connectivity" to "experiential". Traditional TCP/IP-based static architectures reveal three major bottlenecks when dealing with dynamic environments: lack of biological-level adaptive capabilities, weak role interaction support, and absence of emotional dimensions. To address this, we propose a tripartite new paradigm framework: Bio-inspired Network Programming (BINP) demonstrates the evolutionary wisdom of nature, Role-Oriented Networking (RON) reconstructs virtual interaction logic, and Emotion-aware Network Programming ushers in the era of emotional communication—together forming the next-generation network's "intelligence-interaction-emotion" trinity architecture.
