The Revolutionary Perspective of String Theory: From Particles to Vibrations

The core idea of string theory is that the fundamental building blocks of the universe are not point-like particles but tiny "strings." These strings exist in different vibrational modes, each corresponding to a fundamental particle. From photons to quarks, from electrons to neutrinos, all matter and forces are produced by the vibrations of these strings. This perspective not only unifies quantum mechanics and general relativity but also offers a new cosmic vision: a universe composed of vibrations, a harmonious symphony.
