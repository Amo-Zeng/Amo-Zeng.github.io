Comprehensive Management of Ecological Resource Assets: The Integration of Digital Innovation, Community Engagement, and Interdisciplinary Evaluation

In the context of global environmental changes and resource scarcity, the management and protection of ecological resource assets have become a focal point of international society. Traditional management models are gradually showing their limitations, while the integration of digital innovation, community engagement, and interdisciplinary evaluation is emerging as an effective approach to promote the sustainable use of ecological resources. This article will delve into the key dimensions of this comprehensive management method, analyze its advantages and challenges, and propose corresponding strategies to provide new perspectives and ideas for the practice of ecological resource management.
