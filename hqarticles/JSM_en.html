Co-design of JSM with Edge Computing Infrastructure

Traditional modular systems face three key challenges in edge computing environments:

  • Loading jitter caused by dynamic networks
  • Security isolation requirements in multi-tenant environments
  • Memory/computational bottlenecks on resource-constrained devices

Innovative solutions:

  1. Predictive Slicing algorithm
  2. Wasm-based secure cross-sandbox communication protocol
  3. Lite-Container runtime hot-swap technology
