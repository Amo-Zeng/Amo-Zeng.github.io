Multidimensional Evolution of Responsibility: The Reshaping of Responsibility from Biology to the Virtual World

Responsibility, a concept often seen as an external obligation in human society, is, when examined from a biological perspective, actually an intrinsic driving force that propels individuals to continuously evolve and surpass themselves. In the long course of biological evolution, an individual's survival and reproduction often depend on their adaptability to environmental changes, which is largely determined by their sense of responsibility towards themselves and their offspring. The existence of this sense of responsibility prompts individuals to mobilize potential physiological and psychological resources when facing challenges, thereby unleashing their potential and driving the optimization of genes.
