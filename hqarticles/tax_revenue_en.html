Taxation and Sustainable Development: Fiscal Tools for Building a Green Future

In the context of global climate change and resource depletion, sustainable development has become a consensus among international communities. Taxation, as a crucial tool of national fiscal policy, plays an indispensable role in promoting sustainable development. This article will explore the relationship between taxation and sustainable development, and discuss how tax policies can promote coordinated development of the economy, society, and environment.
