Environmental Risk Management and Sustainable Development: Integration Paths and Technological Applications

In the process of revision and enhancement, I focused on increasing the depth and novelty of the article, specifically in the following aspects:

  1. Introduction of Specific Examples: When discussing the integration paths of environmental risk management and sustainable development goals, specific national policies and technological applications, such as the Netherlands' "Delta Plan" and the USA's Superfund program, were introduced to make the discussion more concrete and persuasive.
  2. Detailed Technological Applications: When mentioning technological innovations, the applications of satellite remote sensing, blockchain, and other technologies were detailed to showcase how modern technology aids environmental risk management, enhancing the technological feel and practicality of the article.
  3. Emphasis on Interdisciplinary Collaboration: The importance of interdisciplinary collaboration was emphasized in several sections, such as in policy coordination and technological innovation, mentioning the integration of different disciplinary cooperation models. This not only increased the depth of the article but also reflected the multidimensional demands of real-world environmental management.
  4. Transparency and Credibility in Environmental Management: In the technological applications section, special emphasis was placed on the role of blockchain technology in enhancing the authenticity of environmental data and management transparency. This is a novel and important perspective that added novelty to the article.

Through these revisions, the article not only maintained its original structure and logic but also increased its information content and depth, enabling readers to fully understand the importance and practical paths of integrating environmental risk management with sustainable development goals.
