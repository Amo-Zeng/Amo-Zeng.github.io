The Healing Power of Light Music

Light music provides a sanctuary for the soul with its soothing melodies and gentle rhythms. Studies show that light music can effectively reduce anxiety by lowering cortisol levels. A study by the Cleveland Clinic found that participants' anxiety levels were reduced by an average of 40% with light music.

  • Improved Sleep: A sleep intervention study in Japan showed that listening to light music for 30 minutes before bed for 30 days reduced participants' sleep onset time by an average of 40% and increased deep sleep time by 35%.
  • Enhanced Focus: Research from Stockholm University confirmed that working in a light music environment extended participants' attention span by 25% and improved work efficiency by 30%.

  3. Personalized Music Therapy: Intelligent recommendation systems based on big data analysis can tailor music therapy plans based on individual emotional states and physiological indicators.
