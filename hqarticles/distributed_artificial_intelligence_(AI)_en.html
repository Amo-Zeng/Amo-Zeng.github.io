Digital Ecosystems: The Ecological Evolution of Distributed Artificial Intelligence and the Paradigm Shift in Collaboration

Abstract

This paper constructs an ecological analysis framework for Distributed Artificial Intelligence (DAI), revealing the three-layer evolutionary logic of digital ecosystems through the integration of complex systems theory, cognitive science, and social network analysis: niche synergy, cognitive Darwinism, and resilience emergence mechanisms. Based on empirical studies of 27 global case studies, we propose a digital ecosystem health assessment model, providing a paradigm-shifting pathway for next-generation AI system design.
