Distributed Training and Edge Computing

多智能体和分布式训练（如强化学习）的兴起使AI系统能够在多个设备上协作训练模型。这种去中心化的结构减少了数据集中存储的风险。

The rise of multi-agent and distributed training (e.g., reinforcement learning) enables AI systems to collaborate across multiple devices. This decentralized structure reduces the risks associated with centralized data storage.

New Technologies for Privacy Protection

差分隐私

通过向数据集添加随机噪声，差分隐私确保单个数据无法被逆向推导，同时保留全局统计价值。

联邦学习

模型在本地设备上训练，而非集中传输数据，显著降低隐私泄露风险。

Differential Privacy

Incorporating random noise into datasets ensures that individual records cannot be reverse-engineered, while preserving the overall statistical value.

Federated Learning

Models are trained directly on user devices without requiring data to be centrally transmitted, significantly reducing the risk of privacy breaches.

Advantages of Multi-Agent Collaboration

  • 去中心化：减少单一数据存储节点，避免成为攻击目标。
  • 实时响应：边缘设备直接处理数据（如自动驾驶），降低延迟。
  • 数据本地化：模型在本地训练，减少数据外泄风险。
  • 隐私保护：分布式结构天然增强数据保护。
  • Decentralization: Reduces reliance on centralized data storage, minimizing exposure to attacks.
  • Real-Time Response: Edge devices process data on-site (e.g., in autonomous vehicles), reducing latency and bandwidth usage.
  • Data Localization: Models are trained locally, reducing the risk of data leakage and transmission vulnerabilities.
  • Enhanced Privacy: The distributed nature inherently supports stronger data protection mechanisms.
