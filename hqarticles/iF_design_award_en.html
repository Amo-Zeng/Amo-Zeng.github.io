iF Design Award: More Than an Award, a Connector of Design and Life

Since its inception in 1953, the iF Design Award has evolved from a local German design competition to a global design star. Initially, the iF Design Award aimed to recognize outstanding design work in Germany, but with the deepening of globalization, it gradually developed into a design award with global influence. Today, the iF Design Award not only includes excellent design works from around the world but also promotes the exchange and integration of global design culture through fair, impartial, and transparent evaluation mechanisms.

From "Design Award" to "Innovation Catalyst"

One of the core values of the iF Design Award lies in its establishment of a broad design bridge. It is not only a platform to recognize outstanding design but also a hub that brings together global design forces. By organizing international design exhibitions, seminars, and lectures, iF connects top designers, innovative companies, and academic research institutions. This connection has spawned countless possibilities for cross-disciplinary collaboration, enabling design to move beyond aesthetic and functional considerations and actively respond to social needs, technological advancements, and sustainability challenges.

For example, the iF's collaborative smart home design evaluation project with a well-known technology company not only showcases the latest technological achievements but also promotes the improvement of the smart home ecosystem through close collaboration between designers and engineers. This cross-disciplinary cooperation model enhances the practicality and forward-looking nature of design while also creating new market opportunities for businesses.

Promoting Sustainable Design: Leading the Industry's Future

In the face of global environmental challenges, sustainable design has become an important issue in the design industry. The iF Design Award has keenly captured this trend and incorporated it into the core elements of its evaluation criteria. In recent years, iF has focused more on the environmental performance, resource efficiency, and social responsibility of design works, using the guiding role of the award to encourage designers and companies to practice sustainable development.

For instance, the "Social Innovation" award launched by iF in 2022 aims to recognize innovative projects that solve social problems through design. This award not only showcases the tremendous potential of design in improving quality of life but also sets a new benchmark for global designers, pushing the design industry towards a more responsible and sustainable direction.

From "Niche" to "Mainstream": Breaking Down Barriers Between Design and Life

The iF Design Award is not just a designer and corporate celebration; it is committed to bringing outstanding design works to the public eye, allowing design to truly serve life. Through online and offline exhibitions, product experience activities, and collaborations with retailers, the iF Design Award bridges the gap between design and consumers, allowing more people to experience the charm of design and ultimately promote the formation of a design consumption culture.

Online and Offline Exhibitions: Breaking Spatial Limits, Making Design Within Reach

Every year, the iF Design Award organizes design exhibitions in major cities around the world, showcasing the winning works of the year. These exhibitions are not only a platform for designers and professionals to communicate but also a window for ordinary consumers to understand and experience design. Through intuitive displays, consumers can closely感受 the innovation and beauty of design and understand how design solves practical problems in life.

Product Experience Events: Design is Not Just "Seen" But "Used"

To make design more accessible to life, the iF Design Award often collaborates with brands to launch product experience events. Through these events, consumers can not only see the design works but also personally experience and use these products,感受 how design enhances the convenience and comfort of life.
