Understanding "Ending"

Meanings of "Ending" in Different Contexts

"Ending" in Chinese generally signifies the completion of a process, activity, or phase. It can be used in various contexts, such as the conclusion of a discussion, the completion of a competition, or the finalization of a project.

Emotional Tones of "Ending"

In different contexts, "ending" can carry various meanings and emotional tones. Here are a few examples:

  • Positive: After finishing hard work, you can finally relax.
  • Negative: The relationship has ended, and you feel very sad.
  • Neutral: The meeting has ended, and everyone is free to leave.
