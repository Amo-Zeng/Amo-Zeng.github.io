    Challenges of AI on Data Privacy

    Centralized AI platforms form market barriers by monopolizing user data, increasing the risk of privacy violations.

    Key Risk Points

    • Data oligopolies: Top platforms control 90% of data resources
    • Algorithm black boxes: Strengthen monopolists’ information power
    • Privacy paradox: Users cannot opt-out freely

    Reforms to Balance Data Power

    It is crucial to counteract monopolistic practices through institutional reforms and user empowerment tools. Combining open-source data management with strong legal oversight is essential to creating fairer and more transparent AI platforms.

    User Empowerment Tools

    • Data wallets: Allow users to selectively authorize data usage
    • Differential privacy: Adds noise to datasets to prevent individual identification
