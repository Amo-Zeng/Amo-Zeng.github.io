Food Preservation: A Journey Intertwined with Microbes, Time, and Sustainability

In the pursuit of food, "freshness" has always been a core concept. However, with rapid technological advancements and growing environmental concerns, our understanding of "freshness" is undergoing a profound transformation. This transformation not only involves innovations in food preservation technology but also touches on the role of microbes, the redefinition of time, and the profound environmental impact of food waste. This article will explore these three dimensions to uncover a new perspective on food preservation and propose a more comprehensive and sustainable food system.
