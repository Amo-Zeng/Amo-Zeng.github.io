Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA): Integration and Innovation in Topic Modeling

By combining with advanced technologies such as deep learning, reinforcement learning, and graph neural networks, LDA not only enhances its own topic discovery capabilities but also significantly improves performance across various application scenarios.
