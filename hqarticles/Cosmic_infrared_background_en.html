Gravitational Lensing: The Time Prism of the Universe

Data from the Abell 2744 galaxy cluster indicates that strong gravitational fields not only bend space but also cause light signals to diffract along the time dimension. This phenomenon is similar to diffraction patterns of water waves encountering obstacles, but occurs within the temporal projection of cosmic history, forming unique "time mirror" effects.
