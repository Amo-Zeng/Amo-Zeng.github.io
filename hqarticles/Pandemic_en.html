Prologue: Pandemic as Developer Solution

When Shanghai programmers are trapped by electronic fences scanning "space-time companions," when Berlin cafés fracture consensus in public spaces over vaccine passport disputes, when Tokyo's isolated elderly suffer cognitive regression through physical isolation – what we witness are not mere side effects of pandemic control, but the blue screen of death looming over modern civilization's operating system. This 21st-century global black swan materializes Beck's risk society into an existential experiment – our self-built material barriers and rational doctrines revealing sharp edges of reverse imprisonment under viral mutation.

I. Data Leviathan: Divination Politics in Algorithmic Theocracy

The 2022 health code misjudgment frenzy shattered the myth of technological neutrality. The Zhengzhou red code incident saw 9,000 rural bank depositors' civic identities evaporate in algorithmic black boxes, a perfect illustration of Agamben's "bare life." Daily production of 6.4‰ digital displaced persons in this era of over-surveillance and accidental abandonment validates Byung-Chul Han's warning: "The transparent society kills not secrets, but the breathing space essential to existence."

European vaccine passport debates mirror our era's paradoxes. When data contracts techno-kidnap civic identities under public safety pretexts, Paris court's constitutional ruling touches civilization's ultimate contradiction: as human respiration becomes binary existence certificates, Habermas' "colonization of the lifeworld" achieves quantum leaps. Professor Saito's "digital indulgences" concept resonates – people ritually sacrifice biometrics before cameras, seeking data asceticism to redeem mobility rights usurped by algorithms.
