Frontiers of Digital Governance

Digital Reconstruction of Food Safety Culture

Abstract

This study constructs a three-dimensional analytical framework of "Nostalgia Production-Algorithm Governance-Trust Code" to reveal the transformation mechanisms of food safety culture in the digital era. In 2023, Chinese consumers' digital food safety participation reached 67.3% (CFSA, 2023), while risk perception dispersion widened by 15.2 percentage points year-on-year. The innovative TRUST-E governance model is proposed to provide systematic solutions for digital food safety governance.

Cultural Topology in Technological Evolution

McKinsey reports show a 28% increase in global food industry digital investment in 2022, yet consumer trust index declined by 11%. This research transcends technological functionalism, establishing a dynamic spiral framework of "Deconstruction-Reconstruction-Reinstitutionalization" from a cultural topology perspective.

1. Nostalgia Production: Cultural Politics of Digital Longing

1.1 Memory Reconstruction and Cognitive Discounting

79% of Generation Z encounter traditional craftsmanship through short videos but prefer industrial products. Modified hyperbolic discounting model: δ = α/(1+βD), revealing technological compensation mechanisms of digital nostalgia.

1.2 Landscape Production of Rural Symbols

Ancient brewing technique videos achieved 142% annual view growth, yet less than 7% of live-stream viewers understand actual processes, validating Baudrillard's "hyperreal simulation order" theory.

2. Algorithm Governance: Breaking Cognitive Silos

2.1 Cognitive Narrowing in Personalized Recommendations

83% reliance on food safety algorithms with Information Cocoon Index (ICI) reaching 0.72. Experimental groups showed 39% improvement in risk identification after exposure to diverse information.

2.2 Dual Reinforcement of Risk Perception

Blockchain traceability materializes microbial risks, while multi-dimensional visualization systems improve regulatory response speed by 58%.

3. TRUST-E Governance Model Innovation

  • Transparency Chain: Zero-Knowledge Proof + Consortium Blockchain
  • Responsive Governance: Federated Learning Early Warning System (Response ≤15min)
  • User Empowerment: ISO-Certified 3D Mirror System
  • Ethical Technology: 22-Item Algorithm Ethics Negative List
  • Ecosystem: Quadruple Helix Co-Creation Community

Pilots in Hangzhou and Chengdu demonstrated 27.3% increase in consumer trust index and 41% reduction in regulatory costs.

Cultural Resilience in Digital Civilization

As intelligent algorithms reconstruct food chains, this research emphasizes the necessity of maintaining cultural consciousness and human-centric values, identifying metaverse and quantum computing as future research directions.
