Introduction: From Cost Cutting to Intelligent Optimization

Under the dual challenges of slowing global economic growth and increasing corporate profit pressures, traditional cost-cutting management has shown significant limitations. McKinsey research shows that companies adopting digital cost management can achieve an average operational efficiency improvement of 15-30%. This article systematically explains how to reconstruct the cost management system through technological leverage and innovative methodologies to achieve sustainable competitive advantages.

1. Digital Transformation: Building an Intelligent Cost Control System

An end-to-end data collection system based on Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT), combined with edge computing technology, can establish a cost monitoring network with minute-level response. The global leading chemical company BASF has reduced energy costs by 18% by deploying 20,000+ smart sensors.
