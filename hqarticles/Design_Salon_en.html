The Spirit of Design: A Dialogue Across Time and Emotional Resonance

The Power and Influence of Design

In the vast field of design, each era has birthed unique languages and expressions. The power of design is not only manifested in aesthetic representation but also in its ability to transcend time, resonate emotionally, and lead innovation and responsibility on the path of sustainability.

Design Salon: Dialogue Across Ages

"Design Salon: Dialogue Across Ages" is a feast of dialogue across time, where historical scenarios are recreated and contemporary designers participate, allowing design concepts and practices from different eras to blend and converse.

Design and Emotional Connection Salon

"Design and Emotional Connection Salon" delves into how design resonates emotionally with users. Through neuroaesthetic research, specific design elements can directly affect people's emotional states and behavioral responses.

Sustainable Design Challenge Salon

"Sustainable Design Challenge Salon" focuses on sustainability, aiming to spark innovative thinking and explore the harmonious coexistence of design and the environment. The challenge is set around the most pressing environmental issues, requiring participants to propose environmentally friendly and economically feasible design solutions.

Conclusion

Design is a language that transcends time; it not only connects the past and the future but also builds deep connections with users through emotional bridges. Through these salon activities, we look forward to design continuously evolving across history, emotion, and sustainability, creating not only beautiful but also emotionally moving design works.
