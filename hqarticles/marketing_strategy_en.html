New Trends in Marketing Strategies: Empathy Resonance, Reverse Customization, and Experiential Upgrades

In an era of information overload, consumers are surrounded by vast amounts of information. Brands aiming to stand out can no longer rely solely on product features and price advantages. Increasingly, brands are recognizing that establishing an emotional connection with consumers is the key to winning their hearts and achieving long-term success. The strategies of "empathy resonance" marketing, "reverse customization" marketing, and "experiential marketing" upgrades are effective ways to achieve this goal.
