This linear model struggles to meet the demands of modern cities and industry for efficiency, flexibility, and sustainable development. **The emergence of the energy cloud has completely disrupted this paradigm.** It connects various energy carriers—electricity, gas, heat, renewable energy, etc.—in cities and industry to a unified digital platform. Through data-driven and intelligent algorithms, it achieves precise energy forecasting, dynamic allocation, and optimal configuration.
