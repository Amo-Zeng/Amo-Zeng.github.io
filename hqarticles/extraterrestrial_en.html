In the boundless universe, humanity floats alone, like a tiny speck of dust. This sense of loneliness drives us to constantly look up at the stars, seeking traces of other intelligent life. III. Mental Challenges of Interstellar Contact: Are We Truly Prepared?

In 1965, the science fiction film "Forbidden Planet" filled countless viewers with fantasies about the future: humans could pilot spaceships traveling between stars and engage in dialogue with extraterrestrial civilizations. Today in 2023, we still await the arrival of "first contact." But upon deeper reflection, we must admit: compared to technical preparations, humanity's spiritual readiness may be even more lacking.

What psychological challenges would we face if extraterrestrial life truly visited Earth? First would be the shock to our own existence. When we realize that Earth is not the only planet in the universe, and humanity is not the favored creation of a deity, what psychological impact would this cognitive upheaval bring? Evolution once shook human religious beliefs, and the existence of extraterrestrial life might further dismantle humanity's illusion of its own uniqueness.

Cultural differences would bring significant communication barriers. Linguists have long studied how to communicate with extraterrestrial life, but deeper cultural differences may far exceed our imagination. Different evolutionary paths, different living environments, lead to entirely different ways of thinking and value systems. This is not simple interpersonal communication, but a civilization dialogue across the cosmic divide. Can we accept alien civilizations with open and inclusive minds? Can we maintain reason and self-control in the face of great changes? These questions are more pressing than building spacecraft.

IV. Spiritual Preparation for Interstellar Civilization: The Leap from Mind to Cosmos

Future interstellar contact will not only test humanity's technical prowess but also our cultural sophistication. After all, a true interstellar civilization must first overcome the barriers of the mind.
