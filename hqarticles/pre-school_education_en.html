Early Childhood Education: The Foundation of Future Society

Introduction

Early childhood education, as a crucial component of early child development, has become increasingly significant in recent years from the perspectives of neuroscience, social equity, and the future labor market. Research indicates that the preschool stage is the golden period for brain development, during which the brain exhibits high plasticity and the fastest rate of neuron connection establishment. Therefore, quality early childhood education not only optimizes brain neural networks but also lays a solid foundation for children's future learning, memory, emotional regulation, and social interaction.
