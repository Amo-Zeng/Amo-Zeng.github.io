The Evolution and Paradigm Breakthrough of Neural-Symbolic Integration: From Explainable AI to Human-Like Cognitive Architecture

In the third wave of artificial intelligence development, neural-symbolic integration (Neural-Symbolic Integration, NSI) has evolved from a mere technical fusion to a cognitive computing paradigm. 1. Research Background and Theoretical Positioning

This "perception-reasoning" dual-channel architecture is reshaping the foundational theoretical framework of AI:

  • Cognitive Science Level: Simulating the synergistic mechanisms of the human cerebral cortex (neural networks) and prefrontal cortex (symbolic processing).
  • Computational Efficiency Level: Reducing the parameter search space of neural networks through symbolic compression, achieving 10-100x training acceleration (IBM Research 2022).
  • Engineering Application Level: Meeting the mandatory explanation requirements for high-risk AI systems under the EU AI Act.
