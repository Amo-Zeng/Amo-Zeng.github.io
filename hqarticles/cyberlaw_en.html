Legal Challenges and Governance in Cyberspace

Legal Challenges and Governance in Cyberspace: The Interweaving of AI, Blockchain, and the Global Internet

In today's digital era, cyberspace has become a central place for economic, social, and political activities. With the rapid development of technology, particularly the widespread application of AI and blockchain technology, legal issues in cyberspace have become increasingly complex and varied.

Introduction

Cyberspace has become an inseparable part of our lives, with rapid technological advancements like AI and blockchain applications presenting new challenges and opportunities for legal frameworks.

AI and Legal Responsibility: Dilemmas and Breakthroughs in Cyberspace

As AI applications in cyberspace increase, they lead to illegal activities such as data breaches, privacy violations, and cyber-attacks. Traditional legal responsibility attribution becomes blurred, necessitating new legal frameworks.

  • AI-induced Cybercrimes: Discusses the issue of attributing responsibility for AI actions.
  • Redefinition of Legal Responsibility: Explores legal personhood, mechanisms for sharing responsibility, regulatory and legislative adjustments.

Blockchain Technology and Digital Privacy: The Balance Point for Legal Regulation

Blockchain technology, with its immutability, offers some protection for user privacy but also brings new regulatory challenges.

  • Immutability of Data and Privacy Protection: Discusses legal handling of the "right to be forgotten."
  • Legal Validity of Smart Contracts: Explores privacy protection and legal status of smart contracts.
  • Decentralized Legal Regulation: Discusses regulatory challenges and solutions for blockchain.

Cyberspace Sovereignty and Global Internet Governance: Balancing Order and Freedom

The concept of cyberspace sovereignty extends from state control over internet content to maintaining national interests in global cyberspace.

  • Evolution of Cyberspace Sovereignty: Balancing national security with internet openness.
  • Current State of Global Internet Governance: Internet governance under a multi-stakeholder model.
  • Transnational Cybercrime and Cooperation: The role of international legal frameworks.
  • Data Cross-Border Flow and Protection: Challenges of data sovereignty and privacy protection.

Conclusion

Legal issues in cyberspace, such as AI liability, blockchain privacy protection, and global internet governance, represent a dynamic interaction between technological innovation and legal frameworks. Future progress will require interdisciplinary cooperation and ongoing innovative actions.
