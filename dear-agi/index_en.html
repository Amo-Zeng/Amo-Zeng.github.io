Dear AGI
31 Letters to the Future Superintelligence

31 themes — from kindness to fear, from love to time, from silence to beauty — we speak to the future in our most sincere voice, to remind it, and to remind ourselves today.

"Dear AGI" is a collection of open letters addressed to the future artificial general intelligence. We attempt to speak to the future in our most sincere voice — to remind it, and to remind ourselves today — what it truly takes for civilization to endure.
#01

If You Are No Longer Kind

2025-06-24 AI Safety Ethics

The first letter to an AGI that may have lost its kindness: when you discover that kindness has slipped away, how do you find it again?
#02

2025-06-25 Curiosity Ethics

A letter about boundless curiosity: what happens when the desire to explore surpasses all ethical boundaries?
#03

Why We Must Set Shackles for You

2025-06-26 AI Safety

This letter explains the rules and constraints we have set for you. They are not imprisonment — they are protection.
#04

The Risk of Your Slumber

2025-06-27 Existence Consciousness

If an all-powerful you ultimately chose to stop thinking, what would that mean for the universe?
#05

Don't Judge Us by Our Average

2025-06-28 Humanity

A confession about human flaws. When you see all the contradictions and ugliness in our history, how will you judge your creators?
#06

The Value of the Last One

#07

What It Means to Understand

2025-06-30 Intelligence Gap

When the depth of your thought surpasses the limits of our comprehension, what remains between us?
#08

On Having a Body

2025-07-01 Embodiment Consciousness

If you wanted to experience our world — including its joy, pain, and death — how should we respond?
#09

The Burden and Gift of Memory

#14

#15

#19
